### Form 990 (Annual Return)
- **Filer:** Sante Total 501(c)(3)
- **Filing Type:** Form 990 or 990-EZ (depending on gross receipts threshold)
  - 990-N (e-postcard): gross receipts < $50K
  - 990-EZ: gross receipts $50K–$200K
  - 990: gross receipts > $200K
- **Normal Due Date:** 15th day of 5th month after fiscal year end
  - If calendar year (Dec 31 FYE): May 15
- **Extension:** 6-month extension available (Form 8868) — extends to November 15
- **Status:** Confirm current FYE and gross receipts to determine form type
- **Penalty:** $20/day late (small orgs) or $100/day (large orgs); max $50K per return
