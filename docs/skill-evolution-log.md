## How to Use

- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Skill:** The skill file modified (e.g., `skills/gpu-monitor/SKILL.md`)
- **Change:** What was changed and why — be specific enough that the change is reproducible
- **Result:** What happened after the change — observed improvement, regression, or no change
- **Notes:** Any context that won't be obvious later (e.g., "triggered by failure on 2026-03-28 incident")

New entries go at the top of the table (most recent first). Do not delete entries — use Notes to indicate if a change was reverted.
