## ITC Deadline

The Federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC) and 100% bonus depreciation require the system to be "placed in service" -- meaning fully operational and generating electricity -- before the applicable deadline.

To claim benefits in the 2025 tax year (Form 1065 due Sep 15, 2026):
- System must be in service before July 4, 2026 (conservative target)
- More precisely, before December 31, 2025 (actual 2025 tax year end)

**Important:** The system was NOT in service in 2025. If it is in service in 2026 (before Dec 31, 2026), the benefits move to tax year 2026 and will be claimed on the 2026 Form 1065. The July 4, 2026 internal target simply ensures there is time to confirm the system is operational before the 2026 filing deadline.
