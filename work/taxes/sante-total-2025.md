## 990-N Eligibility

Organizations with gross receipts normally under $50,000 may file Form 990-N (e-Postcard) instead of the full Form 990 or 990-EZ. Sante Total qualifies and has been filing 990-N since at least 2020.

The 990-N requires only basic information:
- EIN
- Tax year
- Organization name and address
- Name and address of principal officer
- Website (if applicable)
- Confirmation that gross receipts are under $50,000

Filed electronically through the IRS e-Postcard system.
