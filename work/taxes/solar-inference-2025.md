## K-1 Pass-Through

Once Form 1065 is prepared, each member (Alton and Aneeta) will receive a Schedule K-1 showing their 50% share of income, losses, and credits. These K-1s flow to their personal Form 1040.

Since the 1065 is extended to September 15, the K-1s will not be available until late summer/fall 2026. This means:
- Personal 1040 may need to be extended (via Form 4868) to October 15 to incorporate the K-1
- Or filed without the K-1 and amended later (not recommended unless necessary)
