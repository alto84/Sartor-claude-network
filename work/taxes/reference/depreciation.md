## Federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC)

- **Code section:** IRC §48 (commercial ITC via LLC)
- **Rate:** 30% of eligible project cost
- **Basis:** $438,829 total project cost
- **Estimated credit:** ~$131,649
- **Treatment:** Dollar-for-dollar reduction in federal income tax liability
- **Entity:** Flows through Solar Inference LLC to members via K-1

The ITC reduces the depreciable basis. When a 30% ITC is claimed, the depreciable basis is reduced by 50% of the ITC amount (i.e., reduced by ~$65,825). This brings the bonus depreciation basis to approximately $372,004 (not the full $438,829).

---

## Bonus Depreciation

- **Code section:** IRC §168(k) as modified by the One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBB, 2025-2028)
- **Rate:** 100% bonus depreciation (immediate expensing)
- **Adjusted basis after ITC reduction:** ~$373,005 (per prior estimates -- verify exact calculation with CPA)
- **Treatment:** Deducted in the year placed in service
- **Entity:** Flows through Solar Inference LLC; creates large paper loss in first year

Note: 100% bonus depreciation was reinstated under OBBB provisions for 2025-2028. This is a significant change from prior law (60% in 2024, scheduled to phase down further). Verify current status with Jonathan Francis.

## NJ SuSI Incentive

- **Program:** New Jersey Successor Solar Incentive (SuSI)
- **Estimated amount:** ~$24,447
- **Type:** Performance-based incentive (per kWh generated over 15 years, paid upfront or annual)
- **Status:** Track application and payment status through Solar Inference LLC

NJ SuSI incentives received by the LLC may be taxable income in the year received. Coordinate treatment with Jonathan.

## Critical Condition: In-Service Date

**All three benefits -- ITC, bonus depreciation, and NJ SuSI -- require the solar roof to be "placed in service" by the applicable deadline.**

The operational deadline driving tax year 2025 treatment is:
- **July 4, 2026** (conservative target to ensure 2025 tax year treatment)

If placed in service after July 4, 2026 but before December 31, 2026, the benefits move to tax year 2026.
