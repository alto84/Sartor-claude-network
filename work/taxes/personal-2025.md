## New Deductions for 2025 (One Big Beautiful Bill Act)

These provisions are new for tax year 2025:
- No tax on tips: up to $25,000 excluded
- No tax on overtime: up to $12,500 excluded
- No tax on car loan interest: up to $10,000 deductible
- Enhanced senior deduction: additional $6,000 for qualifying seniors
