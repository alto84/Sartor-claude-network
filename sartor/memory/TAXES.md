## New Deductions (One Big Beautiful Bill Act, 2025-2028)

These are new for tax year 2025:
- **No tax on tips:** Up to 25,000 in tip income excluded
- **No tax on overtime:** Up to 12,500 in overtime pay excluded
- **No tax on car loan interest:** Up to 10,000 in auto loan interest deductible
- **Enhanced senior deduction:** Additional 6,000 deduction for qualifying seniors
