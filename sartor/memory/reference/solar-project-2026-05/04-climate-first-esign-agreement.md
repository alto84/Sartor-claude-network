# Climate First Bank - E-SIGN Consent

> [!note] This is boilerplate
> Standard E-SIGN Act disclosure that lets the Bank deliver loan/account documents electronically rather than on paper. Accepted at loan application.

## Document basics

- **Title:** Consent for Electronic Transactions, Records, and Signatures
- **Filename in source:** `eSign Agreement.pdf`
- **Bank:** Climate First Bank
- **Bank phone:** (727) 335-0500

## Defined terms

- **Communication** — any communication, notice, disclosure, terms, conditions, agreement, record, statement, or other information provided to or requested from the customer in connection with any Online Service.
- **Electronic Record** — electronic or digital version of any Communication.
- **Online Service** — any electronic product/service offered by the Bank, accessible via internet, web, email, text messaging, or apps.

## Customer agrees to

- Conduct business and enter contracts electronically.
- Receive and provide Electronic Records.
- Use electronic sounds/symbols/processes as electronic signatures.

## Bank's reserved rights

- Provide paper version of any Communication in addition or in lieu of electronic.
- Charge a fee for paper copies (unless prohibited by law).
- Discontinue any e-record fee discount upon withdrawal of consent.
- Terminate the customer's enrollment in an Online Service upon withdrawal of consent.

## Customer obligation to update contact

- Customer must promptly notify Bank of any change in email, mobile number, or other electronic address used for receiving Electronic Records.
- Bank has no obligation to resend communications that bounced as undeliverable.

## Hardware/software requirements

- Modern internet browser with JavaScript enabled
- Email account
- PDF reader
- Sufficient storage to download/save records
- A printer (optional, for paper copies)
- Mobile device with iOS, Android, or Windows Phone
