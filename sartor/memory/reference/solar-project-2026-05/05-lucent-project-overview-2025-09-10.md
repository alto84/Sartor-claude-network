## Process steps Audrey outlines

1. **Site survey** — first and most important step. Requires attic access. Lucent will schedule.
2. **Engineering review** — survey findings sent to electrical and structural engineers; ~1 week.
3. **HOA approval** — if applicable. Customer to provide HOA contact.
4. **PSE&G interconnection application** — submitted with electrical plans. Requires homeowner's insurance declarations page + customer signature. Utility responds in 3–4+ weeks.
5. **Building permit (Montclair Township)** — review timeline 3 weeks to 2 months depending on AHJ workload.
6. **Installation** — once interconnection approval, building permit, and HOA approval (if needed) are in hand.
7. **Township inspection** — usually within a few days of request. Inspector may require attic access or technician/homeowner on site.
8. **PSE&G inspection + meter swap to Bi-Directional** — up to 2 weeks after release.
9. **PTO (Permission to Operate)** issued by PSE&G. **System must NOT be switched on until PTO is issued.**
