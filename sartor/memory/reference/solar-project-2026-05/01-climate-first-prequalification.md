## Key acknowledgments and risk allocations (paraphrased)

- Lucent is solely responsible for project completion. Borrower must resolve installation/performance complaints directly with Lucent. Loan obligation is independent of installation outcome.
- Borrower performed due diligence per "Rooftop Solar: Consumer Protection Recommendations." Lucent is NOT authorized to facilitate the loan application.
- Borrower's eligibility for federal solar ITC is NOT guaranteed by the Bank. The borrower must have sufficient federal tax liability to absorb the credit. Bank does not provide tax advice.
- Insurance: borrower must maintain homeowner's insurance covering full replacement+installation cost or outstanding loan balance, whichever applies; insurance proceeds for damage to financed equipment must be applied to the loan.
- **Scope-change clause:** any alteration to the project that affects financed amount must be agreed in writing, added to the contract price, and approved by the Bank. Borrower is responsible for providing latest contract version.
