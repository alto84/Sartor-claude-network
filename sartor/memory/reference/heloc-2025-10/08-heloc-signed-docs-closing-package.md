## Symmetry Lending mortgage-broker compensation

Disclosed in the closing package:

> 1. Borrower Compensation: You will not pay us any fees, including an application fee or discount points, in connection with this loan.
> 2. Lender Compensation: The lender will pay us a fee as compensation for our services. The amount of the fee will be **2.00% of the initial advance** on your line of credit, plus a **flat fee equal to $499.00**. The amount of compensation that we receive from the lender is not based on the terms or conditions of your loan, such as your interest rate or margin.

Implied compensation (assuming the full $527,000 was the initial advance): 2% × $527,000 + $499 = **$10,539 + $499 = $11,039** lender-paid to Symmetry. Borrower pays nothing direct to Symmetry.
