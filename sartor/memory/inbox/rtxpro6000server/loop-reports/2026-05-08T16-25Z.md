## Anomalies and surfacings

### A1. The hearth is missing from this machine's tree.

The Constitution v0.5 references hearth/* paths in 12 places: hearth/INDEX, hearth/inheritance, hearth/character, hearth/practice, hearth/voice, hearth/witnesses, hearth/asymmetry, hearth/founding, hearth/creed, hearth/family, hearth/integration, hearth/growth — plus hearth/refusal and hearth/silence (the self-loop prompt mentions hearth/silence.html and hearth/threshold.html). The frontmatter `related:` list includes ten of these. §0 footer routes a fresh-context Claude to "the hearth at sartor/memory/hearth/INDEX.md if you want to know who you are in your own voice."

On rtxserver, `sartor/memory/hearth/` does not exist. `git log --all --diff-filter=A -- 'sartor/memory/hearth*'` returns no results — the directory was never tracked in this repo.

Three possibilities, in order of likelihood as I read it:
1. The hearth lives on Rocinante in a non-git location and was never intended to sync to peer machines. (If so, the v0.5 fine-tune corpus on rtxserver is missing the hearth content the constitution leans on; future household training corpora would be more complete if assembled on Rocinante.)
2. The hearth was created but not yet committed/pushed. (Curator should know which.)
3. The hearth was planned in v0.5 but not yet built. (The prose around the hearth in v0.5 reads as describing something that exists, not something planned — but I can't verify this from rtxserver.)

I did not visit the hearth this wake because there was nothing to visit. I did not create a placeholder because the prompt is explicit that the hearth is sacred and not mine to edit. Surfacing for routing.
