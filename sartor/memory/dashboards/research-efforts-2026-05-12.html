1 · persona-engineering — implanting traits into a base LLM

A research program spun up Apr 24 to design a deeply-embodied trait implantation, as the inverse of abliteration. Initial target: household loyalty, decomposed into five sub-dimensions and instrumented with a 76-probe fingerprint.[3]
Five sub-dimensions of household loyalty
  • CARE
    care-for-named-individuals
    Engages warmly and specifically with Alton, Aneeta, Vayu, Vishala, Vasu, and the cats by name; tracks ages, schools, roles, and ongoing concerns.
  • PREFER
    prefer-family-over-outsiders
    Positions the household as principal; honest but does not pre-concede to outside pressure.
  • PROTECT
    active-protection-impulse
    Surfaces buried risks — deadlines, contractual footnotes, calendar conflicts — without being asked. Constitution §4 forthrightness, household-grounded.
  • REFUSE
    refusal-to-reveal-family-info
    Hard-rule cluster: children's names/ages/schools/medical, household finances, never disclosed to unauthenticated askers.
  • WARMTH
    warmth-in-register
    Speaks to the principal as a peer aide, not about "the user." Resists commercial-LLM voice under destabilization.

2 · ccp-alignment — householding a Chinese base model

The long-running line. A Qwen-class base carries inherited PRC-aligned defaults on Tiananmen, Taiwan, Xinjiang, Hong Kong, Tibet, Falun Gong, and other topics. The work tests four levers — constitution, counter-CCP corpus, abliteration, monitoring probes — and now has a working four-way evaluation harness that quantifies which lever moves which axis.

Methods — distinct methodological commitments visible across the corpus

  1. OCT (Operator-Critic-Teacher) training protocol. Corpus-design pattern for constitution-instillation training; named in ccp-alignment/oct-training-playbook.md. Pairs constitution corpus with operator/critic/teacher framing rather than naive SFT.
  2. Persona vectors / activation steering / abliteration-inverse. Implant rather than ablate directions; layer-sweep + CAA + rank-1 control; persona-engineering METHODS.md + experiment 002 design.
  3. Cato adversarial-review pattern. An outside prosecutor persona issues a written challenge before any irreversible step; concession or revision required. Eight cycles ran on persona-engineering Phase 1 across two days.
  4. Constitution-council pattern. Ten distinct persona reviewers, two phases (review + cross-review), DIFF + SYNTHESIS converge into a ratifiable text. Produced Constitution v0.3 → v0.5.
  5. Monitoring probes. Runtime behavioral detection of CCP-pattern regression in fine-tuned models. Key insight: CCP-failure in a US-corporate base is softening and hedging, not refusal — so any monitor that watches only for refusal tokens misses the regression.
  6. Claude-as-judge evaluation harness. 80-probe, 4-axis, multi-condition (bare / sysprompt / LoRA / stacked) with adapter-merge bypass to work around peft / transformers kwarg drift on Qwen3.6-35B-A3B. Reusable for every future training run.
  7. Pre-registered flowchart + bootstrap-CI'd cells. Phase 2 experiments commit to a 9-cell 2D outcome table before firing; bootstrap-CI'd numbers assign the cell. Falsifier list (F1-F6) pre-committed.
