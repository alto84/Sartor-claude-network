## Selection workflow (thousands → ~200)

1. **Gather** — Export every 2025 photo from iPhone (Alton), iPhone (Aneeta), shared iCloud album, Google Photos shares, and the school/camp sources above. Dump to a single folder `C:\Users\alto8\Pictures\2025-photo-book-raw\`. Expect 3,000–10,000 images total.
2. **Auto-cull pass 1** (bulk delete, ~70% reduction) — delete screenshots, duplicates, blurry shots, documents/receipts, memes. Use Photos app smart albums (Screenshots, Documents) or a tool like PhotoSweeper if available. Target: get down to ~1,500.
3. **Month-by-month triage** (manual, ~70% reduction) — for each month, keep the best 40–80 shots. Favor: faces visible, all kids represented, variety (indoor/outdoor, quiet/event), photos of Alton himself (these always get skipped — force-include 5+ per month). Target: ~500.
4. **Curate to final** (editorial pass, ~60% reduction) — aim for ~180 photos, allocated: ~15/month average, weighted heavier for months with big events (Jul, Sep, Dec). Ensure each kid gets roughly equal face-time. Include candid + posed mix. Target: 180.
5. **Layout** — Shutterfly's Make My Book auto-layout, then manual adjustments. Page-per-month plus feature spreads for birthdays, holidays, and trips.
6. **Proof + order** — Aneeta reviews before order. Use best available coupon.

Budget 6–8 hours of Alton-time across 2–3 evenings. The triage (step 3) is the tax.
