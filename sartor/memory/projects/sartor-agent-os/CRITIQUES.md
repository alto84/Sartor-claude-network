# Phase 4 — Six persona critiques of PLAN-v0.1

Each persona reviewed `PLAN-v0.1.md` from a distinct seat, in parallel, fresh context. Per `multi-agent-orchestration` anti-fabrication: do not average; preserve disagreement.

---

## Cato (prosecutor) — Verdict: Revise

Five strongest charges:
1. **§B is theater on the metric that matters.** Two months at 0% occupancy; plan responds with diagnostic ritual, refuses to commit to the price hypothesis. "I don't know yet" wearing a Gantt chart.
2. **§C1 "single highest-impact item" is rhetorical, not measured.** Three sections claim primacy; at most one can be the highest. Per `feedback/prosecutorial-discount-on-constitutional-reframes.md`: the elevation deserves a *double* discount.
3. **Phase boundaries A→B→C are decoration, not gating.** "Phase A is 21 days" but B/C "run in parallel from Week 1." The gate gates nothing.
4. **§H Operating discipline is `complex-project` skill restated.** Padding the plan with framework that justifies the framework.
5. **§K open questions concede the plan does not commit.** Greenlight-yes invoked five times as fig leaf for failure to recommend.

Charges considered and dropped: §D persona-engineering as decoration (false — D2/D3/D5 are concretely operationalized); §J as preemptive immunity (false — most items are genuine constraints from prior decisions).

**Kill-charge:** "No new capability is worth building on this substrate" (§A) vs. funding Phases B/C/D/E/F in same 8-week window. Either substrate-first is true and B–F sequence after A's green window, or the claim is false and §A should be smaller. Plan cannot have it both ways.

Honest version: "Vigil checkpoint reviewed after 30 sessions; rate is whatever the data shows."

Hedge-tic audit: "functions as" count = 0. Migrated cousins = 3: "(subjunctive)," "treat its capability as standby," "(preparatory, not live)." Tic migrated from interior-state to scope-status language.

1. **Calendar/Gmail single-OAuth-family failure.** A1 alarm channel and A2 token rot share `claude_ai_*` OAuth path. Token expiry takes out both anomaly and surfacing channel. Surfaces too late: next morning over breakfast, same as 2026-04-22.

2. **C1 silently bypassed under deadline pressure.** May 15 Wohelo, May 17 Disney ADR pressure orchestrator to rationalize C3/C4 as "Alton-side logistics." Aneeta finds out from Vishala that an agent has been tracking her domain.

3. **Solar ITC attention-starved by D1 fine-tune.** Boring contract-transfer admin loses to intellectually-interesting persona-engineering. Alton's revealed pattern (March-April $0 miss, "behind on Disney"). Surfaces too late: late June, paperwork still pending, no slack.

Missing safeguards:
- Out-of-band escalation when Calendar-MCP IS the channel: SMS, SSH-greppable file, or physical LED.
- Hard pre-flight for rtxpro6000server: 30-min sustained dual-GPU stress test, junction temps, sag-bracket re-torque, AER baseline.

Tone observation: document presupposes assent. Greenlight gates appear as procedural punctuation, not real choice points. The structural fear: this is how a constitutional system slowly drifts into a managed one.
