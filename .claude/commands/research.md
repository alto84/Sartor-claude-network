Launch a structured research effort using the research-effort skill.

Accepts a research topic or question. Walks through: question formulation, team assembly, literature survey, hypothesis & experiment design, iterative execution & evaluation, synthesis, and memory integration. Produces a citable research document, decision matrix, and open questions list.

Usage: /research <topic or question>

Examples:
  /research Can we remove CCP conditioning from Qwen 2.5 7B?
  /research Best local LLM for household inference on RTX 4080
  /research Sonos vs Google Home for TTS morning briefings
