Plan a family trip. The user will specify destination and approximate travel dates. Account throughout for a family of 5: Alton, Aneeta, and three kids ages 10, 8, and 4.

## Step 1 — Research Destination
- Key attractions and experiences appropriate for ages 4–10.
- Best neighborhoods/areas to stay for families with young children.
- Seasonal considerations for the travel dates (weather, crowds, events).
- Entry requirements (passports, visas) if international.

## Step 2 — Accommodations
- Identify family-friendly options with 2+ bedrooms (hotel suite, vacation rental, or adjoining rooms).
- Prioritize: kitchen/kitchenette access (important for the 4-year-old's schedule), pool if possible, proximity to main activities.
- Provide 2–3 options at different price points with pros/cons.
- Note booking deadlines or limited availability windows.

## Step 3 — Day-by-Day Itinerary
Build a full itinerary with:
- Age-appropriate activities for all three kids (not just the older two).
- Built-in buffer time — 4-year-olds need naps, transitions take longer with 3 kids.
- Mix of active and low-energy days.
- Backup indoor options in case of bad weather.
- Meal timing aligned with kids' schedules (early dinners typically better).

## Step 4 — Restaurant Options
- 3–5 restaurant recommendations per day that work for families.
- Note: noise level tolerance (loud is fine), kid menus, outdoor seating.
- Flag any reservations needed and lead time required.

## Step 5 — Transit Logistics
- Getting to/from destination (flights/drive). Note if car seats needed.
- Local transit options (rental car vs rideshare vs public transit with 3 kids).
- Distance and travel time estimates between activities.

## Step 6 — Cost Estimate
- Flights (rough range based on current market if applicable)
- Accommodations (per night × nights)
- Activities (entrance fees, tours)
- Food (daily estimate)
- Transport
- Total range: budget / mid-range / comfortable

## Step 7 — Booking Checklist
Flag items that need booking in advance and when:
- Flights: book X weeks out
- Accommodations: book by {date}
- Any timed entry tickets or reservations

## Output
Save to: `reports/travel/{destination}-{dates}.md`
