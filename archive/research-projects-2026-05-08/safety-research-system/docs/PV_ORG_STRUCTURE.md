  |-- Chairs Safety Review Committee (SRC)

  |
  +-- Head of Patient Safety / Pharmacovigilance
        |-- Chairs Safety Management Team (SMT)

## Governance Bodies

| Committee | Chair | Frequency | Members |
|-----------|-------|-----------|---------|
| Safety Review Committee (SRC) | CMO | Quarterly | Head PS, QPPV, VP Regulatory, VP Clinical Ops, Medical Affairs, Biostatistics |
| Safety Management Team (SMT) | Head of Patient Safety | Monthly | QPPV, all 5 direct reports, Regulatory Affairs liaison |
| Risk Management Committee (RMC) | Director, Risk Management | Quarterly | Head PS, CMO, QPPV, Signal Mgmt, Regulatory, Clinical, Quality |
| DSMB | Independent Chair | Per protocol | 3 independent members (statistician, clinician, ethicist) |

## Operating Model

- **18 internal FTEs + 24 outsourced** (hybrid model)
- In-house: Safety science, medical review, signal management, benefit-risk, aggregate report authoring, quality/compliance
- Outsourced: Case intake/data entry (SafetyFirst Ltd), literature screening (MedSearch Global), LPPV network (PharmaLex), safety DB hosting (Oracle Argus Cloud)

## Key Changes from Previous Structure

1. **Dashboard org chart expanded** from 4 to 7 visible roles under Head of PS
2. **Titles right-sized**: VP PV Operations -> Associate Director; Safety Science Lead -> Director, Signal Management
3. **QPPV clarified**: Designated person with regulatory independence, dotted-line to CMO
4. **Missing roles surfaced**: Risk Management, Aggregate Reporting, PV Quality now visible
5. **Safety Surveillance Director removed**: Function split into Signal Management (detection) and PV Operations (lit screening oversight)
6. **CRO oversight framework**: KPIs defined per vendor
7. **Medical review gap identified**: Safety Physician function under PV Operations
