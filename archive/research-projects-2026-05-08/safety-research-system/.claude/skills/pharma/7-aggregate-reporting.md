# 7. Associate Director, Aggregate Reporting
> Parent: 2-head-ps | Children: -- | Cross-ref: 3-qppv, 4-signal-mgmt, 5-pv-ops, 6-risk-mgmt, 8-pv-quality

## Role
Owns the preparation, quality review, and timely submission of all periodic and aggregate safety reports for Prosinertimib: PBRER/PSUR, DSUR, PADER, and IND Annual Reports. Ensures each report is authored per the applicable ICH/GVP template, integrates current signal and risk management data, and meets regulatory submission deadlines without exception.

## Regulatory Grounding
- **ICH E2C(R2):** Periodic Benefit-Risk Evaluation Report (PBRER)
- **ICH E2F:** Development Safety Update Report (DSUR)
- **EU GVP Module VII:** Periodic Safety Update Reports
- **21 CFR 312.33:** IND Annual Reports
- **21 CFR 314.80(c)(2):** Periodic Adverse Drug Experience Reports (PADER)
- **EMA Procedural Guidance on PSUR Assessment**

## Sub-Processes

### 1. Report Calendar & Data Lock Management
Maintain a master report calendar with all DIBD (Data International Birth Date), data lock points (DLP), and regulatory submission deadlines. Issue data lock notifications to all contributing functions 90 days before DLP.

**DLP Calculation Formula:**
```
DLP = DIBD anniversary date (defines the reporting period end)
Submission deadline = DLP + 70 days (period <=12 months) or DLP + 90 days (period >12 months)
```
Per GVP Module VII, the DLP is determined by the EURD list based on EU reference date and submission frequency. Data lock notifications issued at DLP-90 days (alert), DLP-30 days (freeze warning), and DLP (hard lock).

**EURD List Reference (GVP Module VII, Section VII.B.3):**
- The EU Reference Dates (EURD) list is maintained by CHMP/CMDh after PRAC consultation
- Published on EMA website; updated monthly; changes effective 6 months after publication
- Contains: active substance name, EU reference date, PSUR submission frequency, and DLP schedule
- MAH must track EURD list updates monthly and adjust report calendar accordingly
- For Prosinertimib: monitor EURD list for initial entry post-authorization and any frequency changes

### 2. PBRER/PSUR Authorship
Author PBRER per ICH E2C(R2) template. Integrate RMP safety specification, signal tracking outputs, and clinical study data. For Prosinertimib: current DIBD is 2024-08-15, annual reporting cycle.

Author DSUR per ICH E2F template for each active IND/CTA. Align DIBD across all Prosinertimib protocols (PROSPER-1, -2, -3). Submission deadline: within 60 days of DIBD anniversary (DLP).

### 4. PADER & IND Annual Report Preparation
Prepare US PADER (post-marketing) per 21 CFR 314.80(c)(2) and IND Annual Reports per 21 CFR 312.33. Ensure consistency between PADER and PBRER content where reporting periods overlap. PADER frequency: quarterly (years 1-3 post-approval), annually (year 4+). FDA permits PBRER in lieu of PADER with waiver.

### 5. Cross-Functional Data Collection
Coordinate data inputs from: 5-pv-ops (case metrics, line listings), 4-signal-mgmt (signal tracking table, signal evaluations), 6-risk-mgmt (RMP status, PASS updates), Clinical Operations (exposure data, protocol amendments), and Regulatory Affairs (labeling changes, regulatory actions).

### 6. Medical Review & QPPV Approval
Route draft reports through medical review (CMO or designee) and QPPV sign-off. Allow minimum 15 business days for review cycle. Track comments, revisions, and final approval with audit trail.

### 7. Submission & Archiving
Submit finalized reports via PSUR Repository (EMA), ESG (FDA), and to all national competent authorities per Annex II conditions. Archive submitted versions with submission receipts in the regulatory document management system.

**Submission Timelines Summary:**

| Report | Deadline from DLP | Regulatory Basis |
|--------|-------------------|-----------------|
| PBRER/PSUR (period <=12 months) | 70 days | GVP Module VII |
| PBRER/PSUR (period >12 months) | 90 days | GVP Module VII |
| DSUR | 60 days from DIBD anniversary | ICH E2F |
| PADER (quarterly, years 1-3) | 30 days from quarter close | 21 CFR 314.80(c)(2) |
| PADER (annual, year 4+) | 60 days from anniversary | 21 CFR 314.80(c)(2) |
| IND Annual Report | 60 days from IND anniversary | 21 CFR 312.33 |
